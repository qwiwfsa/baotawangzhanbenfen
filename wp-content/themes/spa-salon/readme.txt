== Description ==

Spa Salon is a beautiful and versatile solution for creating a website for a beauty spa, salon, hair spa, yoga class, or wellness center. Whether you own a spa salon, massage spa, tattoo parlor, tattoo shop, or barbershop, this theme is tailored to your needs. It's ideal for spa owners, dietitians, fitness professionals, and anyone interested in health spa services including physiotherapy and male grooming. The theme is SEO-friendly, guaranteeing that your spa salon, beauty shop, or tattoo studio ranks high on major search engines such as Google. It has a fast, basic, and responsive design that works well on any device, whether it's for a beauty salon or a barbershop.Spa Salon is fully compatible with WooCommerce, making it simple to put up an online store to sell fitness, health, and beauty products. Whether you offer makeup, hair styling, or appointment scheduling, you can easily promote your services with the help of pre-made layouts, sidebars, drop-down menus, and custom widgets. The theme is also translation-ready, which makes it perfect for international beauty spa enterprises. To further customize your site, you may even include custom codes like CSS/JS and shortcodes. Developed using the Bootstrap framework, Spa Salon is easy to use for anyone building a website for a spa, tattoo parlor, or hair salon.
